Hey Kenneth,

We are in the process of creating our complete withdrawal process in Dynamic Forms in Spring of 2026, and we will have more flexibility going forward with when Intent forms open. The closing time would be always the same. The summer intent forms are tricky, and could change. Our Dynamic Form working group hasn't discussed any changing of time frames yet so no changes are official. Flight Path students have been using our standard form since that program came on board. This fall we created a form for that population for the first time.

Here are open and close dates/times for Fall 2025 and Spring 2026.

Fall 2025 Boone, HKY, ASO

Form opened at midnight, on March 24, 2025 , the day after the Spring 2025 withdrawal date ended.

Form closed at 11:59 pm on August 17, 2025 the day before classes began.

Spring 2026 Boone, HKY, ASO

Form opened at midnight on October 24, 2025 the day after the Fall 2025 withdrawal date ended.

Form closes at 11:59 pm on January 11, 2026 , the day before classes begin.

Spring 2026 Flight Path

Form opens at midnight on November 15, 2025 , the day after Fall 2025 F2 closes.

Form closes at 11:59 pm on January 11, 2025 , the day before classes begin.

Note: Summer Intent Forms have opened in the past when the Fall intent opens. They always close at 11:59 pm, the day before the term opens.

I hope this helps. Please let me know if you have questions or need anything else.

Thank you,
